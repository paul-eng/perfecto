PERFECTO

a look at how context instructs meaning in fashion

programming & design by Paul Eng

If you have ever wondered how a trend started, you have at least stumbled into thinking about semiotics. If we look at fashion as a form of semiosis, or sign process, it's easy to see how garments act as signs to communicate ideas. The less obvious part is how the communication eventually becomes part of the idea itself. We'll look at how that has played out in making the perfecto biker jacket into an icon.
